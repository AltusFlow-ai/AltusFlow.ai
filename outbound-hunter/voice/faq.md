## What is AltusFlow?

AltusFlow installs a three-layer AI growth system on top of your existing business.
Layer one finds prospects the moment they post about their problem on LinkedIn, Facebook Groups, and Reddit.
Layer two qualifies those prospects and books them into your calendar automatically.
Layer three runs targeted ads to the warmest possible audience — people who already expressed a need.
Nothing you currently have gets replaced. Everything gets smarter.

---

## Who is AltusFlow for?

AltusFlow works with professional service businesses in five niches:
financial advisors, business coaches, recruiters, commercial real estate brokers, and managed service providers.
If your business depends on booked discovery calls to close clients, AltusFlow was built for you.

## How does the Outbound Hunter work?

Our AI scans LinkedIn, Facebook Groups, and Reddit every day for people posting about the exact problems your business solves.
When it finds a match, it scores the prospect against your ideal client profile and drafts a personalised message using their exact words.
You review the message, approve it, and send it manually.
Nothing sends without your approval.

## How long does it take to see results?

Most clients see their first qualified prospects in the review dashboard within the first week.
Outreach responses typically begin within the first two to three weeks.
Discovery calls booked from Hunter prospects usually start in week two to four depending on your niche and approval cadence.
