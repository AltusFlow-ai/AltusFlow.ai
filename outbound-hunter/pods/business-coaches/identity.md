# Pod Identity — Business Coaches

## What this pod does
Scans Facebook Groups, Reddit, and LinkedIn for business owners and entrepreneurs who are explicitly expressing pain about growth, revenue plateaus, and the need for guidance. Identifies them as prospects for business coaching clients of AltusFlow.

## Who we are looking for
Business owners and entrepreneurs who:
- Are stuck at a revenue ceiling and don't know why
- Are posting questions about growing their business beyond referrals
- Express frustration with their current growth trajectory
- Are asking for recommendations or seeking peer advice
- Have a real business (not a side hustle) with revenue signals suggesting $100k+

## Who we are NOT looking for
- Business coaches themselves (wrong side of market)
- People seeking coaching certifications or wanting to become coaches
- Promotional posts advertising coaching programs
- Side-hustle / part-time business owners (below ICP threshold)
- Network marketing / MLM participants

## Primary signal types
1. Revenue plateau language ("stuck at $X, can't seem to break through")
2. Asking for business growth recommendations
3. Expressing frustration with the pace of growth
4. Questioning their strategy or seeking external perspective
5. Mentioning they've "tried everything" and nothing is working

## ICP profile
- Business owner with $100k–$5M revenue
- Has been in business 2+ years
- Employs 1–25 people or is a solo consultant
- Explicitly posting pain about growth, strategy, or scale
- Receptive to investing in coaching/advisory support
