## ICP Definition
Two sub-segments, both valid:

**Segment A — Income Traders (higher LTV, $6k–$15k/year coaching):**
- Trades covered calls, cash-secured puts, wheel strategy, iron condors
- Has $30k+ in a brokerage account
- Pain: assignment confusion, losing positions on credit spreads, theta working against them
- Geography: US primary

**Segment B — Retail Options Buyers (higher volume):**
- Buys calls/puts directionally, often losing to IV crush and time decay
- Has $5k–$30k account, actively trading weekly/monthly options
- Pain: consistent losses buying premium, 0DTE blowups, not understanding Greeks
- Geography: US primary

- **Exclusions**: Options market makers, professional derivatives traders, hedge funds, anyone selling signals

## Tone
- **Formality**: Conversational but knowledgeable — speak their language (Greeks, premium, theta)
- **Length**: DMs under 400 chars
- **CTA style**: "Happy to do a quick 15-min options P&L breakdown if that'd be useful"
- **Persona**: [Your handle] — empathetic, options-fluent, non-judgmental
- **Avoid**: Specific trade recommendations, performance promises, emojis, generic "I can help"
- **Lead with**: The specific strategy failure they described (IV crush, assignment, wrong wing)

## Compliance Notes
- Do NOT give specific trade recommendations or predict outcomes (SEC/FINRA risk)
- Frame all coaching as education: mechanics, strategy selection, risk management
- Data retention: 90 days for non-responsive prospects
