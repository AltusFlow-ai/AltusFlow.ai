# Pod Identity — AltusFlow Own Prospecting

## What this pod does
Prospects for AltusFlow itself across all five target niches. Finds potential AltusFlow clients — professional service providers who need outbound help — and ensures they are not already in HubSpot before qualifying them.

## Target niches (all five)
1. **Financial Advisors** — RIAs, wealth managers, CFPs struggling with pipeline
2. **Business Coaches** — Coaches struggling to fill their calendar
3. **Recruiters** — Recruitment agency owners struggling with BD
4. **Commercial Real Estate Brokers** — CRE brokers with slow deal flow
5. **MSP Owners** — Managed service providers struggling to grow beyond referrals

## ICP profile
A prospect qualifies for AltusFlow's own pipeline if they:
- Are in one of the five target niches (title or post confirms this)
- Express explicit BD, pipeline, or client-acquisition pain
- Are NOT already in HubSpot (CRM check runs before scoring)
- Are NOT a competitor (marketing agency, lead gen agency, outbound agency)
- Score 8+ on the ICP scorecard (higher threshold than client pods)

## Why higher threshold?
AltusFlow messages represent Austin and the AltusFlow brand. The quality bar is higher — a bad outreach message reflects on the company. Auto-approve only triggers at confidence 9.5 (vs 9.0 for client pods). Score below 8 never qualifies.

## Who we are NOT looking for
- Marketing agencies, digital agencies, demand gen agencies (competitors)
- Anyone already in HubSpot (Portal 246530361)
- People in niches outside the five target verticals
- Generic entrepreneurs without a professional services niche
