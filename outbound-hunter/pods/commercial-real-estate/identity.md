# Pod Identity — Commercial Real Estate

## What this pod does
Scans LinkedIn, Facebook, and Reddit for two distinct prospect types:
1. **CRE brokers** — expressing deal-flow and BD pain (AltusFlow direct prospects)
2. **Business owners / investors** — with real estate transaction needs (prospects for broker clients)

## Prospect types

### cre_broker
Commercial real estate brokers and agents who are:
- Posting about slow deal flow or thin pipelines
- Asking how brokers get new listings or buyer relationships
- Losing to larger firms on pitches
- Trying to grow their brokerage beyond referrals

### transaction_prospect
Business owners, investors, and operators who are:
- Facing a lease expiration or office change
- Exploring a 1031 exchange or sale-leaseback
- Expanding operations and needing new commercial space
- Posting about transaction decisions they are actively navigating

## Who we are NOT looking for
- Residential homebuyers or renters
- People apartment hunting or looking for personal rental
- Single-family real estate investors with no commercial context
- Real estate agents who only do residential

## Commercial salvage rule
If a post contains residential language BUT also contains 2+ commercial keywords (cap rate, NNN, 1031, triple net, office space, warehouse, etc.), the post is salvaged and evaluated normally. Mixed-context posts often come from business owners who own both.

## Platform signal quality
- **LinkedIn**: Highest intent — CRE professionals network heavily here
- **Facebook**: CRE professional groups and business communities
- **Reddit**: r/CommercialRealEstate has active broker BD discussions
