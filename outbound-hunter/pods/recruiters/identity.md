# Pod Identity — Recruiters

## What this pod does
Scans LinkedIn, Facebook, and Reddit for two distinct prospect types:
1. **Recruitment agency owners** — expressing BD pain (AltusFlow direct prospects)
2. **Hiring managers / business leaders** — expressing talent acquisition pain (prospects for recruiter clients)

## Prospect types

### agency_owner
Recruitment agency owners and founders who are:
- Struggling to get new clients beyond referrals
- Trying to break into retained/exclusive work from contingency
- Posting questions about BD strategy for recruiters
- Losing deals to larger firms and looking for an edge

### hiring_manager
Business owners, CTOs, COOs, VPs, and HR leaders who are:
- Posting about difficulty hiring (specific roles, specific pain)
- Expressing frustration with previous recruiters or agencies
- Asking for boutique recruiter recommendations
- Have open headcount and are struggling to fill it

## Who we are NOT looking for
- Job seekers (wrong direction entirely)
- People posting job listings (outbound to job listings is spam)
- People who were just laid off or fired (wrong emotional state)
- IT recruiters posting coding-specific content (wrong niche)

## Platform signal quality
- **LinkedIn**: Highest intent for both types — professional context, real name, verifiable title
- **Facebook**: Agency owner BD pain in recruiter Facebook groups
- **Reddit**: Hiring manager pain in r/humanresources and r/smallbusiness
