The Problem

The Leaky Bucket

You spend thousands on traffic, outreach, and referral cultivation every month. The pipeline fills up — then nothing converts at the rate it should.

Here is where the revenue actually leaks:

Leak 1 — The silent website

A prospect visits at 7 PM Saturday. They have a question. No one answers. By Monday morning they have booked a competitor who had a chat widget that responded in seconds. Your website was a brochure, not a salesperson.

Leak 2 — Slow follow-up

Research shows 78% of deals go to the vendor who responds first. If your team follows up the next business day, you are competing against whoever responded within 5 minutes. Speed is the differentiator, not pitch quality.

Leak 3 — Untracked attribution

You don't know which ad, post, or channel actually generated each client. So you keep spending across everything and can't cut what's wasting money or double down on what's working.

Leak 4 — Missed intent signals

Every day, prospects in your market are publicly posting about the exact problem you solve. They are actively in-market. They have not called you because they don't know you exist yet. That is a prospecting problem with a systematic solution.

Every unanswered question, every slow response, every missed intent signal is revenue leaving the building. AltusFlow plugs all four leaks simultaneously — not as disconnected tools, but as one integrated pipeline.

Do you replace my existing website or CRM?

No. We don't replace — we add to. If you have an existing website, we add the AI chat widget with two lines of code. If you have a CRM (Salesforce, Pipedrive), we sit alongside it. HubSpot handles the AltusFlow reporting layer, and we mirror contacts into your existing system via integration. Nothing gets ripped out.

What if I've been burned by an agency before?

That's the most common thing we hear. Three things are different here: you own all assets from day one, delivery scope is defined before we start with no scope creep, and you get a live dashboard with automated reporting — the data comes from HubSpot, not from us writing a summary of what we think we did.

Can we build it ourselves?

Yes — the tech exists. HubSpot, Meta Ads, LinkedIn tools, website builders. A skilled team could assemble it in 3–6 months. What you are actually buying from AltusFlow is proven architecture already built and tested, templates that deploy in days not months, integrations that already work, and ongoing optimisation without hiring a team. The real question is what your team's time costs and what 3–6 months of not generating demand costs you.

Does the Outbound Hunter send messages automatically?

No. Nothing is sent without a human confirm step. Every morning you see your qualified prospect queue with personalised drafts. You review each one, edit if needed, and confirm what you want sent. This is a non-negotiable design decision — automated sends without review are not something we build.
