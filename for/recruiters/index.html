What the Outbound Hunter Does for Recruiters

Hiring decision makers and frustrated recruiters post publicly about BD being hard, pipelines drying up, and losing to bigger firms. Those posts are intent signals. We find them before your competition does.

Step 01

Scan

Every morning the system searches LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram for posts matching recruiter BD signal phrases — leaders admitting BD is harder than ever, asking how to land retainer clients, or losing business to bigger agencies.

Step 02

Score

AI scores every result on fit — is this person a decision maker? Is the pain specific? Is this a genuine buying signal? Weak matches are filtered silently. Strong matches land in your queue ranked by confidence.

Step 03

Send

A personalised message is drafted referencing their exact post — you're leading with insight about their situation, not a generic cold pitch. Review it, confirm, and it goes to HubSpot. Every touchpoint tracked.
